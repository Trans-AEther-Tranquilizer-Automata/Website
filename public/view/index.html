Field Operations

Modules active on Talos-II. Operational via Discord.

01

Character Roster

View your entire Endfield roster in a visual grid. See every character's portrait, level, element, and rarity at a glance.

02

Character Showcase

Generate detailed character cards showing weapons, gear, skills, and full artwork. Show off your best builds.

03

Event Timeline

A visual timeline of all current and upcoming banners and events, with color-coded bars showing duration and time remaining.

04

Wish Tracking

Import wishes directly from the game and view summaries across all banners. Track pity, history, and pull stats.
