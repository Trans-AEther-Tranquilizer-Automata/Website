# Privacy Policy

**Tata – Discord Bot for Arknight: Endfield**
_Last updated: February 27 2026_

Tata respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect,
how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data.
By using Tata you consent to the practices described herein.

1. **What we collect**
   * **Discord data** – your Discord user ID, username, discriminator, server
     ID, channel ID, and command-related message content.
   * **Game account data** – optional Arknight: Endfield account cookies or
     credentials when you link an account via `/accounts`; used to fetch
     wish history, character info, etc. These are encrypted at rest.
   * **Wishes/history imports** – pull history, wish logs, and similar data you
     import to enable statistics features.
   * **Usage data** – command usage frequency, error logs, and other
     diagnostic information to help us operate and improve the bot.

   We do **not** collect personal information such as IP addresses, or
   payment details.

2. **How we use your data**
   * To respond to your commands and provide the bot’s features within Discord.
   * To persist your game data between sessions.
   * To improve the bot and diagnose errors, including analyzing usage
     patterns and performance metrics.
   * To prevent abuse and enforce Discord’s rules.

3. **Data sharing**
   * We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
   * The external services we interact with include Discord (via its API) and
     the Skport/Gryphline infrastructure at `gryphline.com` when you link an
     Arknight: Endfield account – this is the underlying API provided by the
     Skport social platform.
   * Data may be accessed by the bot’s maintainers and any trusted
     collaborators.
   * If the service is discontinued, your data will be deleted or anonymized
     unless you are otherwise notified.

4. **Retention & deletion**
   * Your data remains stored as long as you or the server administrators keep
     the bot installed.
   * Removing Tata from a server deletes server‑specific settings.
   * Unlinking your Arknight: Endfield account via `/accounts` deletes
     that game data; you can also contact the maintainers for complete data
     removal.
   * Backups may exist for a limited time and are securely destroyed when no
     longer needed.

5. **Your rights & data management**
   You have control over the personal data we hold about you.  To exercise any
   of these rights contact a maintainer or use the provided bot commands.
   * **Access** – request a copy of your stored data.
   * **Rectification** – correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
   * **Erasure** – ask us to delete your data (unlinking or bot removal also
     achieves this for most records).
   * **Restriction** – limit how we process certain types of data.
   * **Objection** – object to processing for particular purposes.
   * **Portability** – request transfer of your data to another service.
   * **Withdraw consent** – revoke permission to process data you previously
     provided.
   * **Complaints** – you may lodge a complaint with relevant data protection
     authorities if you believe your rights have been violated.

6. **Security**
   * We use standard practices (database access controls, encryption for
     sensitive fields, and HTTPS for any external requests) to protect data.
   * Regular security audits and dependency updates are performed.
   * However, no system is completely secure; we can’t guarantee against
     breaches.

7. **Children’s privacy**
   * The bot is not directed at children under the minimum age required by
     Discord’s Terms of Service. If you are under that age, do not use Tata
     without parental permission and follow Discord’s age rules.

8. **Changes to this policy**
   * We may update the privacy policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will
     change, and we may post a notice via the bot or in linked Discord servers.
   * Significant changes will include a one‑week notice period during which you
     may remove the bot if you do not agree.

9. **Contact**
   * For privacy questions or data requests, message the bot’s owner or any of
     the maintainers on Discord; there is no public repository associated with
     the running instance of Tata.
